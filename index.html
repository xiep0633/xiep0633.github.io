About me

I'm a Post-doctoral Fellow in HKUST. Prior to this, I got my PhD from Tsinghua University in June 2020.

I am interested in Partial Differential Equations, especially focus on applied analysis of wave propagation, multiscale method and hyperbolic consevation laws.
